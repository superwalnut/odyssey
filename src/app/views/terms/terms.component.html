Booking Policy

用户可以在任何时候报名或撤销报名， 直至Session开始前5小时，Session开始前五小时内已经报上名的用户将不能撤销， 但可以把你的位子 标注为 “For Sale”， 如果有人来买这个位置那么credit将退回给原来报名的人。

Users can book and cancel a spot at any time until 5 hours before the start of Session after which withdrawing will not be permitted. Instead, you can mark your spot as [For Sale] and if someone comes to buy the your spot, the HBCoin credit will be returned to the person’s account balance who originally signed up.

Book Now! Auto Booking Policy

Auto Booking是一项付费功能， 使用它可以将你或你家庭成员的名字自动固定在每周的接龙里，无需每周再另外接龙，如果在固定期间，你有事不能出席，那么请把你的固定给暂停（Pause） 在创建Auto Booking时， 您必须是HBCoins用户， 而且Credit Balance 大于 {{ autoBookngMiniumCreditRequired | currency}}

Auto Booking is a paid service that automatically pins you and/or your family member's name to the weekly roster, eliminating the need for additional booking every week. If you can't attend during a fixed period, you can pause Auto Booking at any time. You must have a HBCoin Balance equal or greater than $100 to set up an Auto booking schedule.
