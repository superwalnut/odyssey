HBC Open 2023 Competition

规则:

  • 1.小组赛 21分，不换场地，没有setting，每组5进2
  • 2.进10强，两两抽签决定对手，21分,不换场地，没有setting，10进5
  • 3.进5强，两两抽签决定对手，1队轮空直接晋级前三，30分，15 分换场地，没有setting
  • 4.最后三队决出前三

Rules:

  • 1. Group stage: Play to 21 points without changing the venue, no settings. Two teams from each group advance.
  • 2. Top 10: Opponents drawn in pairs, play to 21 points without changing the venue, no settings. Five teams advance.
  • 3. Top 5: Opponents drawn in pairs, one team gets a bye to the top three. Play to 30 points, change venue at 15 points, no settings.
  • 4. Final three teams compete for the top three positions.
